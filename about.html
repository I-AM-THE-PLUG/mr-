About Empower Africa Network

Learn about our mission, vision, values, and meet the dedicated team behind our work

Who We Are

Empower Africa Network (EAN) is a Pan-African non-profit organization dedicated to empowering individuals and communities across Africa through skills-based training, entrepreneurial support, education, and community development initiatives.

Founded with a vision of sustainable development, we work directly with underserved communities to provide practical solutions that address the root causes of poverty and unemployment.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and communities in Africa with skills, resources, and support to drive economic growth and create sustainable livelihoods through innovative programs and strategic partnerships.

Our Vision

A prosperous Africa where every individual has the opportunity to thrive through skills development, entrepreneurship, and community empowerment.

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to becoming a catalyst for change across Africa

2018

Foundation

EAN was founded in Tamale, Ghana with a pilot skills training program for 50 young people, focusing on vocational skills and entrepreneurship.

2020

Expansion & Adaptation

Expanded to 3 additional communities, launched digital skills training, and adapted programs during the COVID-19 pandemic to support local businesses.

2022

National Recognition

Recognized by the Ghanaian government for outstanding community development work and established partnerships with 5 corporate sponsors.

2023

Regional Growth

Extended programs to neighboring West African countries, trained over 1,250 individuals, and supported 320 businesses with a focus on women and youth empowerment.
